Non-ferrous Castings

Non-ferrous alloys do not contain iron in appreciable amounts. Common materials are aluminum, brass, copper and titanium. Non-ferrous castings can be made using sand casting process, investment process, or metal mold process including low pressure permanent mold process and high pressure die casting process (HDC).

Non-ferrous metal castings  known for featherweight factor that lowers overall weight while maintaining structural integrity. These castings have non-magnetic property and resistance to corrosion.

Due to certain inherent advantages of mechanical and chemical properties, the use of non-ferrous castings is increasing day by day. The consumption of these items is by Automobile Industries, machine manufacturing Industries, Textile Industries, Electrical Industries and so on
